10.1 Short mathematical question - Determining rates of diffusion controlled quenching

The rate of diffusion (\(k_d\)) in solution is given by equation (10.1), where \(\eta\) is the viscosity of the solution.

\[\begin{equation} k_d = \frac{8RT}{3 \eta} \tag{10.1} \end{equation}\]

Determine the maximum possible rate of diffusion controlled quenching at 20 ºC in:

  1. water (\(\eta=\) 1.0016 mPa s)
  2. methanol (\(\eta=\) 0.594 mPa s)
